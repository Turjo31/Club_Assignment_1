Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KUET Cyborg.

How do I become a member?

Simply visit us during our regular gaming sessions or contact us through the form. No registration fee is required — all KUET students are welcome!

What games do you play?

We primarily focus on NFS Most Wanted, FIFA, E-Football, and Clash Royale. However, we're always open to adding new games based on member interest.

Do I need to be good at gaming to join?

Not at all! We welcome players of all skill levels — from casual gamers to competitive esports players. Our goal is to build a friendly community, not just winners.

When and where do you meet?

We hold regular gaming sessions every Saturday from 4-7 PM at the Student's Welfare Center, KUET. Tournament schedules vary — check our Events page for details.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership is completely free! We believe gaming should be accessible to everyone. Some special events may have small participation fees for prizes.

Can I organize my own tournament?

Absolutely! If you have a game idea or want to host a tournament, reach out to our Event Coordinator. We're always excited to support member-initiated events.

Do you participate in inter-university events?

Yes! We regularly participate in inter-university gaming championships and esports events. Keep an eye on our Events page for upcoming competitions.

How can I contact the club?

You can reach us via email at kuet.cyborg@gmail.com, call +880 1712 345678, or fill out the contact form. You can also find us at the Student's Welfare Center during session hours.

Still have questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? Feel free to reach out to us directly.
