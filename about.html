About KUET Cyborg

Cyborg is the gaming club for KUET students who love esports, tournaments, and team competition.

Built for campus gamers

Founded with the goal of giving KUET students a strong gaming community, Cyborg brings players together for fun, training, and competitive club events.

Our members come from every department, and we welcome players at every skill level — from casual gamers to tournament veterans.

What we stand for

  • Friendly esports culture
  • Regular practice nights
  • Weekly tournament hosting
  • Skill-building workshops

Why join Cyborg?

There's always something happening at the club, from friendly matches to competitive prize tournaments.

Team events

Build your squad, sharpen coordination, and compete with campus teams.

Esports training

Improve mechanics, strategy, and communication with expert guidance.

New tournaments

Weekly NFS, FIFA, E-Football and Clash Royale events keep the club energized.

Community lounge

Meet fellow gamers, stream matches, and relax in our campus play space.
